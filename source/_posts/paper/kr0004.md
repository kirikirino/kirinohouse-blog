# 成组符号

$$
f(x)=\begin{cases}{ax^2+2}&x\le 0\\{\ln{x-2}}&x>0\end{cases}
$$

```latex
f(x)=\begin{cases}{ax^2+2}&x\le 0\\{\ln{x-2}}&x>0\end{cases}
```

$$
\begin{cases}{a+b=0}\\{a-b=0}\\a=0\end{cases}
$$

```latex
\begin{cases}{a+b=0}\\{a-b=0}\\a=0\end{cases}
```

$$
\begin{array}{|c|c|c|} \hline a&b&c\\\hline 1&0&2\\\hline 2&0&1\\\hline\end{array}
$$

```latex
\begin{array}{\|c\|c\|c\|} \hline a&b&c\\\hline 1&0&2\\\hline 2&0&1\\\hline\end{array}
```

$$

$$

```latex

```
